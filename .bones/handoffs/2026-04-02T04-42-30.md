**createSpecializedTypeAlias** (~280 lines):

### Strategy considerations for the very hard stubs
1. **Interface expansion approach:** Add helper dependencies to the TypeEvaluator interface, keeping them in the closure but callable from extracted code. Already proven with `getTypeOfTypeAliasCommon`.
2. **Co-extraction approach:** Move helper functions alongside the main function into specialForms.ts. Riskier (more moving parts per commit) but reduces interface bloat.
3. **Partial extraction:** Extract the switch dispatch logic but leave the case bodies as calls back to the interface. Hybrid approach.
4. **Consider deferring:** These two functions are the most entangled. May be worth assessing whether the remaining ~830 lines justify the interface complexity.
